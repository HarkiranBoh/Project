To get more people inspired!!

TUT provides technology career inspiration for people by establishing networks of people that work in, or have an interest in technology. By helping to form these networks through discussion and events, TUT encourages mentorship and the support of young people emboldening to work towards a career in technology

I was inspired by my recent trip to San Fransisco about encouranging more people to get into technology driven careers. I want to use my platform to help others gain more knowledge or more of an insight into the world of technology and potentially help others by bringing people together!
